Sharing settings

Decide who can participate in your poll.

  • Everyone with a link can participate

    After creating your poll you will be provided with a link that is unique to your poll and that you can use to invite your participants, e.g. via e-mail. In addition this link is sent to the e-mail addresses you provide Beedle with on this page. Everyone who knows this link will be able to participate. Be aware that everyone can edit every participation when the poll is not protected by a Beedle account.

  • Only invited people can participate

    Only people you have invited via Beedle on this page or later using the admin features can participate in your poll. Each participant receives a link that is unique to this person. The link cannot be shared publicly and participants can only add an entry using their personal link and cannot edit the entries of others.
